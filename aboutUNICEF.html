About UNICEF

  • UNICEF stands for United Nations International Children's Emergency Fund.
  • UNICEF was created in 1946 and works in over 190 countries & territories.
  • UNICEF has spent 70 years working to improve the lives of childern & their families.
  • UNICEF believes - All children have a right to survive & fulfill their potential.
  • It has an Executive Board with 36 members including 1 President, 4 Vice-Presidents and a lot of volunteers.
Source: https://www.unicef.org/
